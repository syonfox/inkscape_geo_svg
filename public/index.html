Welcome to Geo SVG Ink Trap

Geo SVG Ink Trap is an Inkscape plugin for consolidating the SVG standards into a single usable format for GIS professionals. We aim to develop standards through which people can embed geospatial data in an SVG in a transparent, bi-directional manner so that when artists share beautiful maps, analysts can recreate the data. We also strive to provide artists with beautiful tools to create beautiful maps because, in reality, they are often the same people.

Please Contribute to the Discussion

We are looking for feedback and contributions from the community to improve the Geo SVG Ink Trap plugin. Join our forum, participate in discussions, and help shape the future of geospatial data representation in SVG.
